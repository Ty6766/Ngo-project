About

Sidikatu Abeni Foundation is a non-profit organisation that is established and supported by kind hearted individuals, The foundation is supported with money raised from donations by natural persons and corporate
Read more

Our Mission

To further the gospel mission of our Lord Jesus by following the word of God that talks about being a blessing and a help to the helpless people.

Our Core Values

Sidikatu Abeni Foundation does not discriminate against race, color, national, origin, sex, religion, age, disability, political beliefs, sexual orientation material or family status.

Our Vision

To see that the widow get the help that they need in our society.
To ensure that the sick get all possible help needed.
Organizing prayers and providing for orphans.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why does Sidikatu Abeni Foundation exists?

    Sidikatu Abeni Foundation exists to reach out, counsel, support and empower citizens.

  • What was your motivation for starting the Initiative?

    To assists the less priveldged.

  • Where does Sidikatu Abeni Foundation get funds?

    The founder's personal fund and donations from passionate people who love the vision of Sidikatu Abeni Foundation

  • How often do you organise outreaches?

    Every month and yearly, We give out food items and allowances to the elderly.

  • Is Sidikatu Abeni foundation limited to only Nigerians?

    Yes as of now it is only limited to Nigerians.
