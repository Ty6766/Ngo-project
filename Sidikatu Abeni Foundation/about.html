About us

Sidikatu Abeni Foundation is a non-profit organisation that is established and supported by kind hearted individuals,
The foundation is supported with money raised from donations by natural persons and corporate entities.
This foundation is based on the biblical principles of love, This principle was practicalized by the mother of the founder of this foundation and same passed down to the children who are also following her steps and the inspired word of God.

Our Mission

To further the gospel mission of our Lord Jesus by following the word of God that talks about being a blessing and a help to the helpless people.

Our Core Values

Sidikatu Abeni Foundation does not discriminate against race, color, nationality, origin, sex, religion, age, disability, political beliefs, sexual orientation material or family status.
Sidikatu Abeni Foundation exists to reach out, counsel, support and empower citizens.
Putting smiles on people faces is Sidikatu Abeni Foundation first call.

Our Vision

To see that the widow get the help that they need in our society.
To ensure that the sick get all possible help needed.
Organizing prayers and providing for orphans.
Providing for the poor ensuring they get all the help needed.
Children to get help for thier education.
Creating and establishing jobs.
Assist in helping find Jobs.
Establish an healthy home for the elderly.

Our Founder

Founder

My name is Folosade Sofowora (Pastor Mrs). I am a retired social worker, I still carry out my pastoral duties in the ministry i attend. My passion is closely related to Jesus my Lord's passion for humanity, I love teaching the word and practicing it too, A strong believer in the power of true love and mercy in my workplace, ministry and my family and in the community. My vision is to regularly assist people of all ages most importantly the elders who are left uncared for to make sure they are not left out of good care. My joy is derived from seeing people around me happy. I enjoy travelling to diffrent parts of the country to experience the wonders of God on earth, What drives me the most is the word of God, which is one thing i enjoy reading and listening to. Most importantly eternal kingdom is my utmost goal.
